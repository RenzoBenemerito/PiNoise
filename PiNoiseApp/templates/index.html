About


Welcome to pinoise a social suggestion and options serve as a forum for social change. Pinoise comes from the root word "pinoy"(Filipino people) + "noise"(to make suggestion).

Features

Previous Next

Posting

Users can post their ideas, suggestions, and opinions in the platform as long as the post is relevant to the category. They can also comment to the posts.


Connection to the Government

The upvote feature will be essential in this feature. For each category, the post that will receive the higher amount of upvotes, can be recognized by the government but it will still be evaluated by them if the idea, suggestion, and opinion is applicable for implementation.


Upvote

Other users can upvote a post. A user can upvote a post only once, and if the post receives a massive amount of upvote, it can be at the top of the list of posts of the category.

FAQ

What is PiNoise?

PiNoise is a platform where opinions and ideas of the Filipino citizens regarding the issues inside the Philippines are shared and listed together allowing them to express their inner thoughts.

What does PiNoise offer?

PiNoise lets the users post their opinions under a category. A user is give a chance to add another category which will be approved by the admin afterwards. PiNoise also gives the user the right to report a post. A voting system is also developed whre people can up vote or downvote a post.

When can I report a certain post?

A user can report a certain post if it contains an inappropriate category or context.

How can I change my password?

Changing of password requires your previous password followed by entering your new password and confirming it by typing it again which can be done on the Settings.

How can I change my profile picture?

Profile picture can be change on the Settings.
